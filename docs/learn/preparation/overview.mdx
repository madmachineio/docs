Let's start your journey! In this first section, you will get familiar with the basic operations before you diving deeper.

* **Know your hardware**: you will take a quick look at the hardware for the following tutorials. 
* **SwftIO and MadBoard (id)**: you will learn two required libraries for all projects. What is SwftIO used for? What are ids in all codes?
